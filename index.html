Sell with us

Consignment service

Sell select fine jewelry through Inspired By Ian with a straightforward consignment process and clear payout terms.

1. Submission

Send photos and basic details for rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings, or other select fine jewelry pieces.

2. Review

Accepted pieces are reviewed for condition, resale fit, and listing potential before they move forward.

3. Listing

Approved items are photographed, described, priced, and listed through the store.

4. Sale + payout

After the item sells and the applicable return window ends, seller proceeds are paid according to the consignment agreement after sale.

Seller intake form

Consignment submissions are reviewed case by case. Accepted pieces are sold on a 70/30 split, with 70% paid to the seller and 30% retained by Inspired By Ian.

Policies and trust

Store policies

Shipping policy

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days and ship from Vancouver Island, Canada. Tracking details are issued once the order is dispatched, and signature confirmation may be used for higher-value shipments.

Returns policy

Eligible new items may be returned within 14 days of delivery if they are unworn, in original condition, and sent back with original packaging. Pre-owned pieces, consignment items, resized rings, engraved pieces, custom orders, and final-sale items are not eligible for return.

Privacy policy

Inspired By Ian collects contact and order information to process purchases, respond to inquiries, manage seller submissions, and provide customer service. Privacy questions may be sent to dewarianross@gmail.com.

Do you sell both new and pre-owned jewelry?

Yes. Inspired By Ian carries both curated new inventory and selected pre-owned or consignment pieces.

Where do orders ship from?

Orders ship from Vancouver Island, Canada.

How does consignment work?

Clients submit a piece for review, accepted items are listed through the store, and seller proceeds are paid according to the consignment agreement after sale.
